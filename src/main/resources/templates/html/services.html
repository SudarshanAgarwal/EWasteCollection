Services

VALUE RECOVERY :

Our Guarantee !!

All our services and processes are in full compliance with state, and center regulations. All recycling processes as well as downstream vendors have been recommended by third party auditing firms. Your confidential data is properly managed and securely destroyed Ww work in a safe environment.

We work thoroughly and efficiently to maximize your IT asset value and reduce risk. Our transparent process provides detailed information back to our clients at every step throughout the service cycle. We generate a thorough report on every product we receive, whether the items are being refurbished and reused, or dismantled and recycled.
